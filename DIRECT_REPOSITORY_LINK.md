## üì• How to Download (If Found)

1. **Click on the folder** (e.g., "SG ASL Morning 2024-6-9")
2. **Go to:** `FBX/Game Ready/` folder
3. **Download the FBX file**
4. **Convert to GLB:** https://products.aspose.app/3d/conversion/fbx-to-gltf
5. **Save as:** `good-morning.glb` (or appropriate name)
6. **Place in:** `assets/models/asl/`
